## Steps

1. Open front cover.
2. Press and hold $OK$ until it shows `Replace Drum`.
3. In quick succession press $Start \rightarrow Up \rightarrow Down$. Screen will show $01 \rightarrow 00$.
4. Press $OK$ to confirm.
